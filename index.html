What is Cervical Cancer?

Cervical cancer is the fourth most common cancer in women. Cervical cancer is found in the cervix, which is the narrow, lower part of the uterus where it connects to the upper end of the vagina; while ovarian cancer often originates in the fallopian tubes or the ovaries, which are reproductive organs on each side of the uterus in the pelvis.
